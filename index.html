Research Question

How do variations in culture, age, and clothing type change how women view clothing as an expression and its effect on their self expression?

Background: Changes in culture have extreme effects on how people dress and it can confine them to certain stereotypes. For instance crop tops are a newer fashion trend that has evolved from more modest dressing from the early 1900s. This has changed how cultures view modesty, and how women are allowed to express themselves.
